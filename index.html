Stories Arabia is a full service Video Production and Post- Production Company based in Atlanta, Georgia.

Since 2007, Stories Arabia has spearheaded some of the most cutting edge content across the continuum of the industry. From feature films to corporate videos, branded content to t.v. shows, music videos to 360 executions: we’ve done it all, and then some. If you need video of any kind, we’re the seasoned experts who will bring it life.

Our winning combination of best-in-class technology and completely kick-ass team members is unrivaled. We pride ourselves on staying ahead of industry trends, both on set and in the editing bay. For us, it’s never a question of “what’s the latest & greatest?” It’s only ever “what’s the next great idea, and how can we bring it to life?”

Located in north Atlanta, our state-of-the-art facility is both our command center and our home away from home. It’s where every stage of the video magic happens–from conceptualization, to production, to post. We firmly believe in importance of making work fun, both for our team and for our clients. When you come by for a meeting, you’ll see it firsthand.

How we do it

Concept Development

1.Understand the project's objectives, target audience, and message.
2.Brainstorm ideas for the storyline, visuals, and animation style.
3.Develop a clear concept and script that aligns with the goals.

Pre-Production

Create a detailed storyboard or animatic that outlines the visual progression of the animation. Develop a shot list, detailing camera angles, transitions, and key scenes. Plan the audio elements, including voiceovers, music, and sound effects.

Audio Production

Record voiceovers or dialogue based on the script. Add background music and sound effects to enhance the storytelling. Ensure that the audio elements complement the visuals and contribute to the mood.

Animation

Start animating according to the storyboard and shot list. Pay attention to timing, spacing, and fluid motion. Experiment with different animation techniques to bring characters and scenes to life.
